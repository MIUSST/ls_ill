DLS.Point

Class which saves the DLS informations of a single count at a defined scattering angle Angle.

Sample Properties

  • Refer to SLS.Point

Properties

  • G : Intensity Correlation - 1 (array).
  • dG: Error of previous (array).
  • Tau [ms] : Lag Time array.
  • Instrument : Class of type [[Instrument]]

Methods

  • fit('Method'): Fit correlogram with Fit-Methods.
  • fit_raw('Method') : Fit raw correlogram with Fit-Methods.
  • correct_G() : normalizes G(t) to yield G(0) = 1.
  • invert_laplace(): inverse laplace -> call C-code by M. Hennig.

Create Instance example

sample = SLS.Sample('Path', path, ...
'Protein'      , Protein      , ...
'Salt'         , Salt         , ...
'T'            , T            , ...
'n'            , n            , ...
'n_set'        , n_set        , ...
'C'            , c            , ...
'C_set'        , c_set        , ...
'Cs'           , cs           , ...
'Instrument'   , 'ALV'        );

% possible additional arguments: 

'filegroup_index' , filegroup_index , ...
'start_index'     , start_index     , ...
'end_index'       , end_index       , ...
'number_of_counts', number_of_counts, ...

The additional arguments can be supplied but are not necessary.
filegroup_index defines the block of data with the same amount of counts per angle (integer).
The number_of_counts variable defines the number of counts per angle which are to be used from the autosave data.
